On February 3, 1956, with A No. 21 Notary Notary Tan 21 Sioe Bar in Semarang the foundation of the Indonesian Christian teacher's education college (yptpgki) with the chairman of S.M.A. Pasariboe, who later established the Indonesian Christian teacher's the educational college to be inaugurated on November 30, 1956, at the Kalitaman Salatiga Hotel with O.Nothohamidjojo as the first Dean. The Learning Module for Preventing and Handling Sexual Violence (PPKS) in the Higher Education Environment was developed in an effort to prevent sexual violence in the tertiary environment through learning.
